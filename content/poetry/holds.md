title: "Holds"
slug: holds

Water is water because the weakest bond holds.\
A proton leaning toward oxygen — that lean, nothing else, holds.

A fermata means *stop*. The tempo is yours now.\
The orchestra waits. The soloist decides what holds.

They find the groove in a room of strangers.\
Nobody started it. Nobody owns it. The groove holds.

The keystone of an arch is always falling —\
toward a center nothing reaches. The arch holds.

When you've held someone long enough, let go.\
Something stays. The shape of it, in the arms — that holds.

Count every natural number. In order. Forever.\
The smallest infinity is also the most patient. Aleph holds.
